1.0 INTRODUCTION

Gramma Federal 5300 41-31 13T is an east lateral Three Forks first bench well located in
SW SW SEC 31, T153N, R100W in McKenzie County, North Dakota. The primary pay
zone extended from 13’-24’ below the top of the Three Forks. This pay zone was picked
for its production potential and quality of reservoir rock. The objective was to steer the
well within the defined pay zone and within the legal requirements of the state.
Directional drilling was performed by RPM and MWD services were performed by Ryan
Directional Services. Mark Miller and Adam Harris were the primary well site geologists;
providing geo-steering and mud logging from Columbine Logging, Inc.
